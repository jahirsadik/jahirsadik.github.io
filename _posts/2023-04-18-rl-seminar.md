Presentation slides
======
Presentation slides for our seminar on Reinforcement Learning to the CAILab, CSEDU were designed to outline key concepts in reinforcement learning, ranging from introductory definitions to advanced topics. The seminar covered foundational aspects like the Markov decision process (MDP), value-based methods such as Q-learning, policy-based approaches, deep reinforcement learning with neural networks, and multi-agent systems.
